THE VISITOR

JACE GESMONDI

is a Rhode Island based illustrator inspired by the genres of horror, dark fantasy, and science-fiction. He has worked in mediums such as gouache, acrylics, graphite, and charcoal, but mostly sticks to pen and ink, and digital. His work contains shades of ambiguity, heavy detail, and the recurring themes of worship, death, and the otherworldly. He is currently studying illustration at the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth.
